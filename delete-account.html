Account Deletion

Delete Your TimeRow Account

How to delete your account and synced data for TimeRow (com.nasirakram.timerow)

Steps to delete your account

  • Open the TimeRow app
  • Go to Settings
  • Select Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion

What gets deleted

  • Your TimeRow account (Firebase Authentication)
  • Your cloud-synced tasks and related records stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Analytics identifiers associated with your account (where applicable)

Tasks stored locally on your device can also be removed by deleting tasks in the app or uninstalling the app.

If you can’t access the app

If you are unable to access TimeRow to delete your account (for example, you lost access to your device), contact us and we’ll help:

Email: developernasirakram@gmail.com
